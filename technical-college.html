Message from the Chairman

At Guru Harkrishan Technical College, Fazilka, we firmly believe that quality education is the strongest tool for social and economic empowerment. Our institution is committed to providing value-based, affordable and skill-oriented education aligned with modern industry requirements. We take special pride in supporting students from economically weaker and underprivileged sections of society. Through government-recognized scholarships, including 100% Scholarship for SC Students, we ensure that financial constraints never become a barrier to education. Our focus is not only on academic excellence but also on personality development, employability skills, and ethical values, so that our students emerge as confident professionals and responsible citizens. I warmly invite students and parents to be a part of our institution and build a successful future together.

Er. Rakesh Bhusri
