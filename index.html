About the Society

Guru Harkrishan Rai Educational Society (Regd.), Fazilka is a registered non-profit organisation established in 2001 under the Societies Registration Act, 1860. The Society has been consistently working in the fields of education, skill development, healthcare support and social welfare with a vision to empower youth and strengthen communities.
